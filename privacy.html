privacy policy

Introduction

At Soles, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect information to provide you with a seamless shopping experience. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card numbers and billing addresses, which are securely processed by our payment partners.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system to enhance your browsing experience.
  • Usage Data: Pages you visit and products you view, which help us improve our website and product offerings.

How We Use Your Data

Your data allows us to:

  • Fulfill your orders and manage your account.
  • Personalize your shopping experience and recommend products.
  • Communicate with you about promotions and updates, with your consent.
  • Improve our website's functionality and security.

Data Security

We take your data security seriously. We implement a variety of security measures, including SSL encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal information. Your payment details are tokenized and processed by a trusted third-party provider to ensure maximum security.

Your Rights

You have the right to control your personal data. You can:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your personal information at any time.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request the deletion of your account and personal data.

For any requests or questions, please contact our support team.
